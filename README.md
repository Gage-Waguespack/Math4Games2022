****R E A D M E****

**Once you have downloaded the .zip file, Extract the files and then launch the .exe file to play the game!**


In this game you fly around in a space ship and shoot enemy space ships with lazers.
    
    Use W, A, S, and D keys to move your ship around.
    Use the up arrow to shoot your lazers.

*If you get hit by a lazer you will die and lose the game.*

*If you shoot all the enemies the you will win.*


*****HAVE FUN!!!*****
